Customer-facing product boundary for the restaurant website.

## Responsibilities

- Landing pages
- Restaurant branding
- Public menu
- Reservations
- Gallery
- Contact
- Marketing pages
- Ordering experience
- Customer-facing interactions

## Exclusions

- Dashboard
- Decision engine
- Analytics
- Insights
- Owner tools
